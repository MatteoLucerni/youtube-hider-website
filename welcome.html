Welcome to Youtube Hider

Your YouTube experience, cleaned up and distraction-free.

What does it do?

Hide Watched Videos

Already seen a video? It disappears automatically based on how much you've watched. No more cluttered feeds.

Remove Shorts

Tired of Shorts popping up everywhere? Remove the Shorts shelf, tab, and navigation entirely.

Filter Low Views

Set a minimum view count and automatically hide videos that don't meet your threshold.

Skip Intros & Recaps

On Netflix and Prime Video, intros and recaps are skipped automatically. Sit back and enjoy.

How to customize your settings

1

Find the extension icon

Look for the Youtube Hider icon in your browser's toolbar (top-right area). If you don't see it, click the puzzle piece icon and pin Youtube Hider.

2

Click to open settings

Click the Youtube Hider icon to open the settings popup. Here you can toggle features on and off, adjust thresholds, and more.

3

Use the floating button on YouTube

You'll also see a small floating button on YouTube pages (bottom-right corner). Click it for quick access to the main settings without leaving the page.
